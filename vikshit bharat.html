Welcome to Viksit Bharat

Viksit Bharat 2047 is the vision to transform India into a developed nation by the 100th year of independence. The mission focuses on economic growth, innovation, sustainability, and empowering every citizen to reach their potential[4][5].

New Startup

Innovative Startups

India is witnessing a boom in startups across sectors like fintech, healthtech, agritech, and edtech. Young entrepreneurs are driving change with technology and creativity, contributing to the vision of a developed India.

Support Ecosystem

Government initiatives like Startup India, Atal Innovation Mission, and Make in India are fostering a robust ecosystem for startups, providing funding, mentorship, and global market access.

New Technologies

Artificial Intelligence

AI is revolutionizing sectors like healthcare, agriculture, and manufacturing, making processes smarter, faster, and more efficient.

Green Technologies

Renewable energy, electric vehicles, and sustainable practices are at the core of India's commitment to environmental sustainability and a cleaner future.

Space & Defence Tech

ISRO and DRDO are making India a global leader in space exploration and defence technologies, supporting national security and scientific advancement.

Indian Defence System

  • Modernization: India is rapidly modernizing its defence forces with advanced technology, indigenous weapon systems, and cyber capabilities.
  • Make in India: Major focus on indigenous manufacturing through projects like Tejas fighter jets, Arjun tanks, and BrahMos missiles.
  • Global Peace: India is a key contributor to UN peacekeeping missions and maintains a strong, responsible defence posture.
